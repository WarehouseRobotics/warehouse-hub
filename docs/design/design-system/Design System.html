01 · Principles

Swiss operational, warm and cozy
Paper-on-paper neutrals, hairline rules, one warm ember accent. Engineering precision at the grid, conversational softness at the edges. Built for humans & agents working the same book.
Precision
4px grid. Tabular numbers everywhere a value matters. Monospace for IDs, amounts, times. No ambiguity.
Calm
Warm neutrals instead of cold slate. Shadows reserved for overlays. Motion under 200ms and purposeful.
Agentic
Agents are first-class. Their activity is visible. Their tool calls are readable. Handoff to humans is obvious.
Deterministic
Surface the state model. Status fields, audit trails, idempotency keys — out in the UI, not hidden.

07 · Motion

Short, confident, purposeful
Three durations, two easings. Use motion to confirm state changes (toggle, selection, optimistic updates) — never decoratively.
Dur-1 · 120ms
Hover, focus, pressed
Tactile. Confirms the cursor.
Dur-2 · 200ms
Tab switch, toast enter
Structural. Confirms the view.
Dur-3 · 320ms
Drawer, agent dock
Spatial. Confirms the layout.
